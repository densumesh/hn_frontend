• " to match a particular sequence of terms "ring"
  • - to ensure a word won't appear in the result set ring -wedding,
  • by:USERNAME to only show results from a particular author(s),
  • by:-USERNAME to exclude particular author(s),
  • site:WEBSITE to only show results from a particular website(s),
  • site:-WEBSITE to exclude particular website(s),
  • story:ID to filter by story ID,
  • points>NUMBER or points<NUMBER to filter by points,
  • comments>NUMBER or comments<NUMBER to filter by number of comments,
  • date>mm-dd-YYYY or date<mm-dd-YYYY to filter by date,
  • type:{post-type} where {post-type} is one of story, comment, ask, show, job, or poll
  • OR can be done with the keyword search mode (all terms OR'd together with BM25 scoring) combined with the " operator to require certain terms.
